Tesla Model 3

The Tesla Model 3 is an all-electric sedan offering impressive range, cutting-edge technology, and a sleek modern design. It is one of the most popular electric vehicles worldwide.

Key Features:

  • Up to 358 miles of range per charge
  • 0-60 mph in as little as 3.1 seconds
  • Full Self-Driving capability
  • Minimalist interior with a large touchscreen
